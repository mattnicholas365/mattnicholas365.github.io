RAP & Cultural Capability – FAQs

Common questions from Australian organisations

What is a Reconciliation Action Plan (RAP)?

A RAP is a practical plan for action built on relationships, respect and opportunities to advance reconciliation within your organisation.

How long does a RAP usually take?

Typically 8–16 weeks depending on scope, stakeholder engagement and governance steps.

Do you support RAP reporting and evaluation?

Yes — we help define measures, collect evidence and produce reporting aligned to your RAP commitments.

Are you Supply Nation registered?

Yes. We are Supply Nation registered and support Indigenous procurement and supplier diversity targets.
