Cultural competence training

What is cultural competence training?

At 2Ways Consulting, our training is specifically designed to enable an understanding and build strong relationships with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples within your organisation and with the communities you engage. Our training enhances awareness, equips your team with practical skills, and empowers your business to confidently and respectfully interact with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples

"Cultural competence training is essential for enabling an inclusive and respectful work environment. It equips individuals with the knowledge and skills to navigate cultural differences and build stronger, more collaborative teams."

How we conduct cultural competence training

We have developed training modules that address your organisation's specific needs and industry context, promoting a nuanced understanding of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultures.

  • Tailored Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultural programs
    We design training modules that reflect your organisation’s specific needs and industry, ensuring relevance and impact when building understanding of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultures.
  • Practical engagement skills
    Our training goes beyond theory, focusing on actionable strategies and practical skills that enable your employees to engage effectively and respectfully with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples and communities.
  • Contextualised learning experiences
    Utilising a blend of engaging eLearning and interactive workshops, we ensure learning is relevant and applicable to real-world scenarios your team will encounter when interacting with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples and communities.
  • Enhanced cross-cultural communication
    Participants will gain tools and insights to improve communication, build trust, and enable, more inclusive working relationships with Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander colleagues, partners, and stakeholders.
  • Measurable impact and growth
    Our programs are designed to facilitate tangible shifts in knowledge, attitudes, and behaviours, contributing to a more culturally adept and harmonious workplace that respects and values Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultures.

How we can help your organisation

At 2Ways Consulting, we are passionate about supporting organisations to build inclusive, culturally safe, and respectful workplaces that value and empower Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples. Our cultural competence training services are designed to enable genuine understanding and embed sustainable change.

If you’re ready to elevate your team’s cultural competence and create a more inclusive environment, we’d be delighted to partner with you. Contact us today to start the conversation.
