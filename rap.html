Reconciliation Action Plans (RAP) - Enhancing Cultural Awareness and Fostering Reconciliation

What is a Reconciliation Action Plan (RAP)?

A Reconciliation Action Plan (RAP) is a strategic document that outlines an organisation’s commitment to contributing to reconciliation in Australia. It includes practical actions that will drive the organisation’s contribution to reconciliation both internally and in the communities in which it operates.

"Reconciliation Action Plans are more than just documents; they are commitments to fostering understanding, respect, and positive relationships between Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples and the wider Australian community. Implementing a RAP demonstrates an organisation's dedication to making a lasting impact through cultural awareness and reconciliation."

How We Conduct RAP

Our RAP service is designed to guide organisations through the development and implementation of their RAP. Here's how we conduct the RAP process:

  1. Reflect: Understand your organisation's current cultural awareness and identify areas for improvement. We conduct a thorough assessment to gauge your starting point.
  2. Develop: Create a customised RAP that outlines clear goals, actions, and deliverables. Our experts work closely with you to ensure the plan is tailored to your specific needs.
  3. Implement: Put the plan into action with our support. We provide the necessary tools, training, and resources to help you achieve your RAP goals effectively.
  4. Engage: Foster engagement within your organisation and the broader community. We facilitate workshops and seminars to promote understanding and participation.
  5. Review: Regularly review and assess the progress of your RAP. We help you track milestones, measure success, and make necessary adjustments to stay on track.
  6. Report: Document and report on your RAP achievements. We assist in preparing comprehensive reports that highlight your successes and areas for further growth.

How We Can Help Your Organisation

Our RAP service is designed to not only demonstrate our capabilities but also to provide a seamless and supportive experience for our clients. Here’s how we can help your organisation:

  • Expert Guidance: Our team of experts will guide you through every step of the RAP process, ensuring that your plan is effective and impactful.
  • Customised Solutions: We understand that every organisation is different. That's why we tailor our RAP services to meet your specific needs and goals.
  • Training and Resources: We provide comprehensive training and resources to equip your team with the knowledge and skills needed to implement the RAP successfully.
  • Community Engagement: We help foster meaningful engagement with the communities you operate in, building strong relationships and promoting reconciliation.
  • Ongoing Support: Our commitment doesn't end with the delivery of your RAP. We offer ongoing support to help you achieve long-term success and continuous improvement.
